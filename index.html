About

Kerala Blasters FC, fondly known as "The Blasters," is a professional football club based in Kochi, Kerala, India, that competes in the Indian Super League (ISL), the top tier of Indian football. Established in 2014, the club has rapidly ascended to become one of the most popular and well-supported teams in Asia, boasting a passionate fan base known as the "Manjappada" or "Yellow Army."

The Blasters' journey began during the inaugural season of the ISL, where they made an immediate impact, reaching the finals but narrowly losing to ATK in extra time. Despite the heartbreak, the club's attacking flair and unwavering spirit ignited a passionate fan following, establishing a formidable reputation in Indian football. Their home matches at the Jawaharlal Nehru Stadium in Kochi became a spectacle, drawing record-breaking crowds that roared with every touch, every tackle, every goal.

Driven by the unwavering support of their loyal fans, the Blasters continued to challenge for glory in the following seasons. They reached the finals twice more, in 2016 and 2022, but ultimately fell short of the ultimate prize on both occasions. Despite these setbacks, the club's determination and the fervent support of their fan base have cemented their status as a powerhouse in Indian football. The Blasters remain a force to be reckoned with, aiming to bring home the coveted ISL championship in the years to come.

Kerala Blastres fans

Kerala Blasters FC, one of the most popular football clubs in India, boasts a passionate and dedicated fan base known as Manjappada, meaning "Yellow Army" in Malayalam. Renowned for their unwavering support and electrifying atmosphere at home games, Manjappada has earned a reputation as one of the most vibrant and enthusiastic fan groups in Indian football.

Founded in 2014, Manjappada has grown rapidly, expanding its presence across Kerala and beyond. With over 100,000 members worldwide, the fan club has established branches in each of Kerala's 14 districts and in 12 other Indian states. Additionally, Keralites residing in over 60 countries have joined the ranks of Manjappada, demonstrating the global reach of their unwavering support for Kerala Blasters FC.

Manjappada's dedication to their team is evident in their consistent presence at home matches. During the inaugural season of the Indian Super League (ISL) in 2014, Kerala Blasters FC recorded an average attendance of 49,000, the highest in the league. By 2015, Manjappada's organizational efforts further boosted attendance, with the Blasters averaging 52,000 fans per match, setting a new ISL record.

Their unwavering support extends beyond mere attendance numbers. Manjappada is known for their synchronized chants, colorful displays, and unwavering passion throughout the match, creating an electrifying atmosphere that has become synonymous with Kerala Blasters FC home games. Their energy and enthusiasm have not gone unnoticed, as Manjappada has twice been awarded the Indian Sports Honours Fan Club of the Year award, in 2017 and 2019.

Manjappada's impact extends beyond the boundaries of the football field. The fan club actively engages in community service initiatives, demonstrating their commitment to giving back to society. They have organized blood donation camps, distributed food and clothing to the underprivileged, and supported educational programs for underprivileged children.

In conclusion, Kerala Blasters FC's Manjappada stands as a testament to the unifying power of sport and the unwavering passion of fans. Their dedication, enthusiasm, and commitment to community service have set a high standard for fan clubs across India and beyond.
